The conversation begins with the prospective student inquiring about PhD admissions at the university. The university representative confirms that registrations/applications are open online. The admission process involves clearing the entrance exam, followed by topic selection and research, not a pre-entrance research paper submission.

The student expresses a need for clarity on admission chances and inquires about the process if their marks in the entrance exam are low. The representative confirms that admission is primarily through the entrance exam. If the entrance exam is not cleared, the student might have to inquire about management quota admissions, which would require a visit to the university. The representative advises visiting the university for any further detailed information. The student's last date for application is the 19th.
